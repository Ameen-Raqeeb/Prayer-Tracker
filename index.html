متابعة الصلوات

Fajr الفجر

"Whoever prays Fajr in congregation, then sits remembering Allah until the sun rises, then prays two rak'ahs, will have a reward like that of Hajj and Umrah."

Dhuhr الظهر

"The time for Dhuhr prayer begins when the sun has passed its zenith and a man's shadow is equal in length to his height, until the time for Asr prayer comes."

Asr العصر

"Whoever misses the Asr prayer, it is as if he has lost his family and property."

Maghrib المغرب

"The time for Maghrib prayer is when the sun has set and the twilight has not disappeared."

Isha العشاء

"If it were not to be a hardship on my Ummah, I would have ordered them to delay the Isha prayer until the first third of the night."
